Safety & Supervision?

Your child's safety is our absolute priority. We follow strict BSA protocols:

  • Two-Deep Leadership: Minimum of two registered adults at every activity.
  • No One-on-One Contact: Strictly prohibited in person, via text, or online.
  • Background Checks: Every adult leader undergoes comprehensive background checks and YPT training.

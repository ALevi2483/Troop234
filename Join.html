Join Troop 234

If you are looking for a troop where Scouts camp, learn real skills, and grow into leaders, Troop 234 is a solid fit. We are a Scouting America troop in Chesapeake, Virginia. We focus on outdoor adventure, leadership, and character.

Who can join

  • Boys ages 11 to 17
  • Or Boys who have completed 5th grade and are at least 10 years old
  • New Scouts are welcome any time of year

What Scouts do in Troop 234

  • Campouts and outdoor activities
  • Hiking and basic outdoor skills
  • Cooking and teamwork in patrols
  • Service projects in the community
  • Leadership opportunities that are Scout led
  • Advancement support from trained adult leaders

What parents should know

  • You do not need Scouting experience to join
  • Adults support the program, but Scouts lead the troop
  • We keep safeguarding youth a priority
  • Costs vary by registration, uniform, and campouts

How to get started

  • Step 1: Come visit a meeting
  • Step 2: Meet the Scoutmaster and adult leaders
  • Step 3: We walk you through joining and next steps

Ready to visit?

Send us a message and we will get you the next meeting details.

About Troop 234

Troop 234 in Chesapeake, Virginia has proudly served the community for more than 35 years.

Chartered by the Rotary Club of Chesapeake, we welcome Scouts from all faith backgrounds.

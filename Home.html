Adventure. Leadership. Service.

We’re so glad you’re here! Troop 234 has proudly served the Chesapeake community for over 35 years, helping Scouts become leaders, adventurers, and citizens of character. Our program is designed to build confidence, foster friendships, and create lifelong memories—one campfire at a time.

Whether you're new to Scouting or continuing your journey, we invite you to explore what makes our troop special. We're more than a group—we're a family.

Scout-Led Values

Troop 234 is youth-led, empowering Scouts to plan adventures, meetings, and community service with trusted adult mentors.

Family Involvement

We believe in strong family partnerships. Parents serve as volunteers, committee members, and supporters every step of the way.

Service & Community

Giving back is part of our DNA. Our Scouts engage in service activities that make a difference locally and beyond.

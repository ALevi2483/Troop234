Welcome to Troop 234 in Chesapeake, VA

Adventure. Leadership. Service.

We’re so glad you’re here! Troop 234 has proudly served the Chesapeake community for over 25 years, helping Scouts become leaders, adventurers, and citizens of character. Our program is designed to build confidence, foster friendships, and create lifelong memories—one campfire at a time.

Whether you're brand new to Scouting or continuing your journey, we invite you to explore what makes our troop special. We're more than a group—we're a family.

Scout-Led Values

Troop 234 is youth-led, empowering Scouts to take the lead in planning meetings, adventures, and community service projects with adult mentorship.

Family Involvement

We believe in strong family partnerships. Parents are encouraged to participate as volunteers, committee members, and supporters at every stage.

Service & Community

Giving back is at our core. Our Scouts regularly engage in service activities to make a difference in Chesapeake and beyond.

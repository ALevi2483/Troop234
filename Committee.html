Troop 234 Committee Overview

The Troop Committee plays a vital role in supporting the Scouts and Scoutmaster by handling administrative tasks and ensuring the troop operates smoothly. Key responsibilities include:

  • Organizing fundraising activities
  • Managing troop finances and records
  • Assisting with membership and recruitment
  • Supporting advancement and recognition programs
  • Coordinating transportation and logistics for events

Regular committee meetings are held to plan upcoming activities, discuss troop needs, and ensure alignment with Scouting principles. All parents and guardians are encouraged to participate and contribute to the success of Troop 234.

Troop 234 Committee Overview

The Troop Committee plays a vital role in supporting Scouts and adult leaders by managing the administrative and logistical aspects of the troop. Committee members work together to ensure smooth operations and continued success.

  • Organize fundraising activities
  • Manage troop finances and records
  • Assist with membership and recruitment
  • Support advancement and recognition programs
  • Coordinate transportation and logistics for events

Committee meetings are held regularly to plan upcoming activities, review troop goals, and ensure alignment with Scouting principles. All parents and guardians are welcome to participate and contribute to the success of Troop 234.

Troop 234 has returned from an action-packed week at Woodruff Scout Camp in Blairsville, Georgia. From white water rafting and merit badge progress to fireworks and wildlife encounters, the Scouts made the most of every moment.

The week was filled with highlights including:

  • White water rafting on Class through 3 rapids (with only a few splashdowns!)
  • Monte and Bryson completing the 1-mile swim challenge
  • Hiking treks including 5- and 8-mile routes
  • Jackson C. recognized for aiding an injured Scout
  • Lawrence completing his board of review for the rank of Star scout
  • Evening fireworks show and troop bonding

Despite several nightly visits from Peanut the skunk and his growing family, Scouts managed to dodge any unwanted sprays by moving through camp with caution and stealth.

The week wrapped up with packed vehicles, cleaned campsites, and one final group memory under the stars. Scouts returned home tired, proud, and full of stories they won't forget.

Please Congratulate Our Newest Wood Badge Participants!

Please congratulate Katie Anderson, Barry Anderson, and Adam Levine on the successful completion of Wood Badge Leadership Training – CST15-596-25, held May 02–04 & May 31–June 1, 2025. They are now working on their ticket items—personal goals that will directly benefit Scouting and their local communities.

This year’s course was proudly led by Troop 234’s very own Kate Sklat as Course Director, with Assistant Scoutmaster Charlie Tate also serving on staff. We are proud to see our troop so strongly represented at this premier adult leadership training!

The next Wood Badge course is scheduled for May 1–3 and May 30–31, 2026. If you haven’t taken Wood Badge yet, we highly recommend signing up! It’s an incredible opportunity to grow as a leader and better serve our Scouts.

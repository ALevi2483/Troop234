New to Troop 234? This is everything you need to know to get started.

First Meeting Info

  • When: Mondays, 7:00–8:30 PM
  • Where: Covenant UMC, 2004 Dock Landing Blvd
  • Uniform: Not required first night
View Calendar Contact Leadership

Your First Night

  • Arrive 10 minutes early
  • Bring a water bottle and notebook
  • Parents stay first 15 minutes
  • Scout will be paired with a guide or patrol leader

What Parents Should Know

  • Meetings last about 90 minutes
  • Parents do not need to stay
  • Campouts happen monthly
  • Communication is through Slack (adults) and BAND (scouts)

How to Join

  1. Attend a meeting
  2. Complete BSA registration
  3. Submit medical forms (Parts A & B)
  4. Pay dues

Required Forms

  • Parts A & B required for all events
  • Part C required for 72+ hour events

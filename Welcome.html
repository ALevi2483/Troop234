How Scouting America Works

Scouting is a youth-led program that encourages leadership, outdoor skills, and community service...

Troop Organization

Troop 234 is organized into patrols... Key leadership roles include:

  • Chartered Org Rep
  • Scout Committee Chair
  • Scoutmaster
  • Assistant Scoutmasters
  • SPL / ASPL
  • Patrol Leaders / APLs
  • Advancement Chair, Treasurer, Secretary...
Advancement and Ranks

Scouting offers a pathway for personal and skill development. Scouts progress through ranks by completing requirements and displaying leadership.

  1. Scout
  2. Tenderfoot
  3. Second Class
  4. First Class
  5. Star and Life
  6. Eagle Scout
Uniforms

Class A (Formal): Shirt, pants, neckerchief, belt, socks (optional)

Class B (Casual): Troop t-shirt, modest solid pants or shorts

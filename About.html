About Troop 234

Troop 234 in Chesapeake, Virginia has proudly served the community for more than 25 years, offering youth opportunities to explore the outdoors, develop leadership skills, and grow in character.

Founded on the core principles of Scouting — Duty to God and Country, Duty to Others, and Duty to Self — Troop 234 is chartered by Covenant United Methodist Church and welcomes Scouts from all faith backgrounds.

Our leadership is fully committed to supporting the Boy Scouts of America’s non-discrimination policies. We believe every young person, regardless of race, religion, or background, should have the opportunity to experience the adventure and values of Scouting.
